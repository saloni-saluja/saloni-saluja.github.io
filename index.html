About Me

I am passionate about generative AI, data science, artificial intelligence, and technical communication. With expertise in software development, machine learning, cloud computing, and technical writing, I specialize in translating complex technologies into actionable insights. My experience allows me to bridge the gap between intricate technologies and clear, accessible documentation, ensuring that both technical and non-technical audiences can fully understand and benefit from advanced innovations.

I’m particularly excited about the transformative potential of generative AI, large language models (LLMs), and AI-driven automation. My work includes prompt engineering, fine-tuning models, implementing retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and deploying agentic AI solutions. I’ve also worked on AI-powered documentation projects to improve user experience and streamline workflows, making these powerful technologies more intuitive and accessible.

With a strong foundation in machine learning, data science, and cloud technologies, I’m skilled in tools like Scikit-learn, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, as well as platforms such as IBM Watson Studio and Google Colab. I’m also experienced with cloud environments like IBM Cloud and Google Cloud, version control using GitHub, and programming languages such as Python, Java, C++, C, and C#.

Beyond technology, I’m deeply committed to mental health, accessibility, and ethical AI. I believe that as we push the boundaries of innovation, we must ensure our work is responsible, inclusive, and benefits all people.
